01 About

Behind the code & score


I'm Rachelann — a Software Engineering student at Grand Valley State University with a focus in Intelligent Systems, and a genuine love for two very different kinds of systems: the ones you build in code, and the ones composers spent centuries perfecting on the page.

I started playing piano at five years old, long before I ever wrote a line of code. Music has always been the constant — through theory classes, ensemble rehearsals, and competition stages. I began formal voice training in August 2025 after auditioning at GVSU, proving it's never too late to add a new language to your stack. Outside the classroom, I serve on the Dean's Student Advisory Council, mentor and tutor incoming freshmen, and work as an IT Intern.

I believe in exploring boldly and failing loudly. Whether I'm debugging a program or preparing a Baroque aria, I'm drawn to the same thing: understanding why something works the way it does, and then making it better.
